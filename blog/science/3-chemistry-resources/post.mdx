Chemistry is the study of matter and the changes it undergoes. It is a fascinating and important subject, but it can also be challenging to learn. If you are looking to improve your understanding of chemistry or just want to learn more about it, there are many resources available online that can help.<!--truncate-->

Here are the top three resources to learn chemistry for free online:

### 1. [Khan Academy](https://www.khanacademy.org/science/ap-chemistry-beta)

Khan Academy is a non-profit organization that provides free, high-quality educational resources to students around the world. Their chemistry lessons cover topics from basic chemical concepts to advanced topics such as thermodynamics and organic chemistry. They offer a variety of resources, including videos, interactive exercises, and practice problems.

- **Price**: Free
- **Paid Option** : No
- **Difficulty Level**: Beginner
- **Flexible schedule**: Yes

**Who should take this course?** This is a good option for complete beginners who want to prepare for high school or university-level courses.

Link to the course - [Crash Course Biology](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QnQe0xW_JY4&list=PL3EED4C1D684D3ADF)

### 2. [Learn with Dr. Scott](https://learnwithdrscott.com/course/free-basic-chemistry/)


- **Price**: Free
- **Paid Option** : No
- **Difficulty Level**: Beginner
- **Flexible schedule**: Yes

**Who should take this course?** This is a good option for beginners who want to understand the major ideas in Biology.

Link to the course - [Concepts of Biology](https://openstax.org/details/books/concepts-biology)

### 3. [General Chemistry I: Atoms, Molecules, and Bonding](https://www.edx.org/course/atoms-molecules-and-bonding) - EdX

edX is an online learning platform that offers a wide range of courses on various subjects, including chemistry. They have courses on topics such as general chemistry, organic chemistry, and biochemistry. Many of their courses are offered by top universities and institutions and are often taught by experienced professors. Some courses may require a fee to access, but many are available for free.

- **Price**: Free
- **Paid Option** : Yes
- **What the paid option offers**: Certificate of completion and graded assignments
- **Difficulty Level**: Beginner
- **Flexible schedule**: Yes

**Who should use this resource?** While EdX does provide general classes on Biology, their resources on more specific subdisciplies are top-notch. We highly recommend learning general Biology until you find what interests you most, and see if EdX has a class on it!

Link to the course - [Various Biology Courses](https://www.edx.org/learn/biology)

### Final Thoughts

These are just a few of the many resources available online for learning chemistry. Whether you are a student looking to improve your understanding of the subject or just someone who is curious about chemistry, these resources can help you learn more about this fascinating and important field.
